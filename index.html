What You Get

Everything a creator needs

Built from the ground up for audio creators. Every feature is designed around sound first.

Upload Any Audio

MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC — upload any format. Add cover art, title, and description. Your track is live in seconds.

Build Your Following

Follow creators, get notified when they drop new audio, and grow your own audience organically through great content.

Discover New Sound

A personalised For You feed and trending discovery tab surface audio you'll love — from genres you follow to new ones you'll discover.

Direct Messaging

Connect directly with creators and fans. Send voice messages, text, and share audio tracks in conversation.

Series & Episodes

Organise your content into series. Perfect for podcasts, albums, or audio diaries. Listeners follow the series, not just you.

Creator Profile

A beautiful profile page, shareable link, and full audio portfolio — your own page on the web at audiovibe.co/@you.

Simple by Design

Up and running in three steps

No learning curve. Create an account, upload your first track, and start growing your audience today.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up in seconds. Choose a username that becomes your permanent audiovibe.co URL.

2

Upload Your Audio

Tap upload, pick your file, add cover art and a title. Your track is live and playable immediately.

3

Share & Grow

Every track gets a share link with a rich preview card. Share anywhere — Instagram, WhatsApp, X — one tap opens the app.
