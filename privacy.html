Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the AudioVibe app for mobile devices, developed by Alpha Tech Labs Ltd as a free service.

Effective: 18 April 2026  ·  App: AudioVibe  ·  Developer: Alpha Tech Labs Ltd
01

Information We Collect

The Application collects information you provide when registering and using the app. Registration is not mandatory, but some features require an account.

The Application allows you to upload audio recordings, which are stored on our servers and may be visible to other users based on your privacy settings.

We also collect certain information automatically: device type, unique device ID, IP address, operating system, browser type, and usage data.

02

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to operate and improve the Application, provide support, send important notices, and deliver relevant content and features.

The Application may use automated systems to recommend audio content based on your listening activity, solely to improve your in-app experience.

03

Location Data

This Application does not gather precise real-time location information from your mobile device.

04

Third-Party Services

Only aggregated, anonymised data is periodically transmitted to external services to help improve the Application.

The Application uses the following third-party service which has its own privacy policy:

Google Analytics for Firebase — Privacy Policy

We may disclose your information:

  • As required by law or in response to legal process
  • To protect rights, safety, or investigate fraud
  • With trusted service providers bound by this privacy statement
05

Opt-Out & Account Deletion

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application via your device or the Google Play Store.

To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at the email address below.

06

Data Retention

We retain user-provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. Automatically collected information is retained for up to 24 months.

07

Security

We implement physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards including JWT-based authentication, rate limiting, encrypted connections, and certificate pinning. No system can guarantee complete protection, but we take every reasonable precaution.

08

Children's Privacy

AudioVibe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If discovered, such data will be deleted immediately. Parents or guardians should contact us if concerned.

09

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you by updating this page. Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
